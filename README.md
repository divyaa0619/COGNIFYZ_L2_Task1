🍽️ Task 01 - Table Booking & Online Delivery Analysis

**🎯 Objective**

**To analyze restaurant services such as table booking and online delivery, and study their relationship with ratings and price ranges.**

**🛠️ Tools Used**
    • Python
    • Pandas
    • Matplotlib
    • Seaborn
    • Google Colab

📌 Steps Performed


1.Loaded the dataset
2.Explored table booking and online delivery columns
3.Calculated percentage of restaurants offering table booking
4.Calculated percentage of restaurants offering online delivery
5.Compared average ratings of restaurants with and without table booking
6.Visualized average ratings
7.Analyzed online delivery availability across price ranges
**
💡 Key Insights


• Only a portion of restaurants provide table booking and online delivery
• Restaurants offering table booking tend to have higher ratings
• Online delivery availability varies across price ranges
• Customer convenience services impact restaurant performance
